## Overview

12-month job search permit for recent graduates (Dutch or top foreign universities). Free work authorization during search year.

---

## Requirements

### Required Documents
Diploma, passport, proof of savings, health insurance. Apply within 3 years of graduation.

---

## Application Process

1. Apply to IND with diploma 2. Show financial means 3. Receive 1-year permit 4. Can work freely 5. Must transition to work permit by end of year

## Duration & Renewal

- **Initial Duration**: 12 months (1 year)
- **Renewable**: No

## Rights & Restrictions

### Work Rights
Full work authorization during orientation year (no work permit needed)

### Restrictions
Non-renewable, must apply within 3 years of graduation, temporary purpose (does NOT count toward 5-year PR), must transition to work visa by end
